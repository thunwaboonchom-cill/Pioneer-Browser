# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of Pioneer Browser, pledge to make participation in our community a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and welcoming.
- Giving constructive feedback.
- Respecting differing viewpoints.
- Helping new contributors learn.
- Acting professionally in discussions.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or insults.
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any illegal or malicious activity.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors who violate these guidelines.

## Contact

If you experience unacceptable behavior, please open a private discussion with the project maintainers.
